Our Story

AIS Concepts was founded with a simple belief: great architecture balances innovation, sustainability, and human experience. Based in Kenya, we work locally and internationally to deliver design excellence across commercial, residential, cultural, and mixed-use projects.

From concept to construction, we create spaces that inspire and function. Our designs respond to context, climate, and culture—whether a high-rise tower, a net-zero residence, or a cultural landmark.

Founder story

AIS Concepts grew from a notebook of sketches and site visits: a belief that architecture in East Africa could be both globally current and deeply local. Today we mentor young designers, invest in research, and keep the studio small enough to stay hands-on with every client.

Design mission

We design buildings and places that improve everyday life—measuring success in daylight, quiet, durability, and delight. Sustainability and innovation are not add-ons; they are embedded from the first sketch.

Studio culture

Peer reviews every week, shared material libraries, and an open desk policy between architects and visualization artists. We protect focus time for deep work and celebrate craft—from model-making to on-site walks.
